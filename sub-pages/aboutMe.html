Hi! Once agin, my name is Daniel Kareti. I created this page to provide a more in-depth look at my background, goals, and experiences.

Education

I completed my undergrad at Santa Clara University in June, 2024 majoring in Computer Science and Engineering. In the fall of the same year, I began a Master of Engineering program at Dartmouth College, majoring in computer engineering.

Career Goals

My career goal is to bridge low-level hardware systems with high-level intelligent software, building robust and scalable technology solutions. I’m also passionate about open-source collaboration and continuous learning.

Experience

My experiences involved both hardware and software technologies, beginning with C and C++. I used these two languages to learn core fundamentals and data structure algorithms. I then began to utilize Python for machine learning and natural language processing. In terms of hardware systems, I became extremely familiar with the Vivado design suite by Xilinx. Using this tool, I programmed in both Verilog and VHDL. My experience with hardware was augmented through my time working as a research assistant at Santa Clara University's Internet of Things Lab. Throughout the years, I worked and grew familiar with different operating systems; I am comfortable with Ubuntu, Linux, macOS, and Windows.
